A Little About Us

Here at PlantYolk, we value health and enjoyment over anything. We believe that a healthy beverage should be affordable and tasty, two things most other health drink companies never achieve. That's why we do everything we can to ensure you're getting the many benefits of eggplant juice for a cheap price.

My name is Taylor Johnson, and I started PlantYolk from my backyard in Atlantic City, New Jersey. I always knew eggplant juice was healthy and tasted great, so I started making bottles for my friends to try. I kept experimenting with different fruits and flavors to achieve the perfect drink. Eventually, my friends told me we were sitting on a gold mine if I was willing to sell it. I was never in it for the money, but I decided to try.

The next thing I knew, my whole city was drinking PlantYolk eggplant juice. People ran to me on the streets telling me how much better they felt. If I could change Atlantic City with a drink recipe, maybe I could change the world. Ever since that moment, it has become my mission to sell PlantYolk across the world. It would be a dream for me if you wanted to help my mission and purchase some eggplant juice. Post to socials with #YolkAroundTheWorld and help show me that my dream is becoming realized.
